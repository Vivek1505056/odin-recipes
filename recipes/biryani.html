Chicken Biryani

This is a homemade classic recipe, Delicious rice and chicken to satisfy your cravings

Chicken biryani is a flavorful and aromatic dish that is popular in South Asian cuisine, particularly in India and Pakistan. It is a one-pot dish that combines marinated chicken, basmati rice, and a variety of spices.

  • Chicken
  • Rice
  • Spices
  • oil
  1. Marinate Chicken with spices
  2. Cook the rice
  3. Cook the chicken
  4. Put them together at the right time and mix them to finish the biryani
