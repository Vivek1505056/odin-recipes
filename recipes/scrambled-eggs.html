Scrambled Eggs

Nutrious, high protien breakfast

Scrambled eggs are a classic, versatile breakfast dish made by cooking beaten eggs in a pan until they are set into soft, creamy curds. I love scrambled eggs. I have been eating them for breakfast every single day for over 5 years and they were a staple in my diet when I lost over 50 pounds!

  • Eggs
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Butter or Oil
  1. Mix the Egg yolks until it becomes one fluid substance
  2. Put pan on light heat and add butter or oil
  3. Add the liquid and keep mixing and dragging the eggs until cooked
  4. Salt and pepper at the end to your liking
